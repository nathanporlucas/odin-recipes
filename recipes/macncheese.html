Baked Mac n Cheese

Homemade baked mac and cheese. The perfect side dish recipe for just about any meal, including holidays!

Ingredients:

  • Elbow pasta
  • Unsalted butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Whole milk
  • Half and half
  • Freshly shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Freshly shredded Gruyere cheese
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Paprika

Steps:

  1. preheat the and grease a 9 x 13 inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. While the noodles are boiling grate the cheese. When the cheese is grated put both varieties in a large bowl and toss to mix.
  3. Melt the butter.
  4. Remove sauce from heat and add salt.
  5. Pour cheese over the pasta and stir until fully coated.
